Services

It's really just like a dating app!

Easy Interaction

After browsing the animals, if you see an animal that you'd like, you can either click on the like button and even request a date(a time to meet the little fella).

Latest Feeds

You can get the latest news about the shelter! Get notified of the new animal available or any other relevant feeds on the shelter immediately.

Secure Login (both sides)

A trustworthy system for both sides. Only shelter admins can manage the animal listings. Unauthoried users cannot create, update, or remove animal listings. Only registered users can like an animal and use the feature of date. Shelter gets real-time reliable data for only the "serious applicants".

About

The Story of PetFect

  • September 2021

    Our Humble Beginnings

    It started out with just choosing a Capstone Project for the Fall Quarter of 2021. Then, with Alice's past encounter with the difficulty of adopting a cat (lost in the long description and no where to find out more information except for calling), PetFect was born as a result. A "dating app" for animal adoption with ease.

  • October 2021

    The Team Was Born!

    The team consist of Alice Li, Chang Li, and Xiaoshi Zeng was born. All of us are animal lovers. In fact, Chang is a mother of two cats.

  • October-December 2021

    Mission of PetFect

    The name came from the core idea of the app which is to obtain the “perfect pet” for prospective owners. The mission of the website is to match shelter animals up with future owners with ease. The focus is on the ease.

  • October-December 2021

    Decision To Utilize Flask and Python

    The decision was to utilize Flask and Python to build our website as they provides the flexibility of website application framework that many does not.
